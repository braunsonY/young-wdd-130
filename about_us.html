A Brief History

CinemaCity started as a dream Beaudry Young, one of the founders, thought about in highschool for a business project. He told the idea to his brother Braunson, another movie enthusiast, and they pledged to turn the dream into a reality. After they graduated from college they opened the one and only CinemaCity in Spokane Washington in 2018. After the initial opening CinemaCity found immediate success. The founders are planning on opening other locations in the future.
